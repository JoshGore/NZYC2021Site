Guidelines

The aim of the conference is to develop the spiritual understanding and commitment of those attending and strengthen friendships around the Word of God. Our fellowship is based on the Word of God and living a life according to it.

Our conference hosts have the responsibility of ensuring an environment meeting the conference objectives. We are to respect their instructions and decisions at all times.

  1. Please attend all organised activities on time so that the conference can run smoothly.
  2. Remain within the conference site boundaries unless granted specific permission to leave by one of the hosts.
  3. Men are not allowed in women’s rooms and vice-versa.
  4. Personal electronic devices can distract us and detract from the atmosphere of the camp, please keep their use to an absolute minimum.
  5. Please do not bring alcohol, tobacco, or other recreational drugs to the conference.
  6. Please dress respectfully for scriptural sessions and modestly in general.
  7. Formal attire is preferred for the memorial meeting and head coverings are considered scriptural for sisters.
  8. Modest shorts/pants are allowed for women during recreational afternoon activities. Everyone is expected to dress tidily for the evening meal and subsequent activities.
