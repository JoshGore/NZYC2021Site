Speaker

Bro. Ben O'Grady, Christchurch North

Topic - Jonathan


Jonathan was a man destined for greatness but who submitted faithfully to the hand of God in his life. His life was a tapestry of bravery, sadness, friendship and tragedy. It remains an example of humility, faith, hope and love.

Throughout his life Jonathan enacts a remarkable type of faithful Israel as God’s people who enter into an everlasting covenant of kindness with the Beloved. We also see glimpses of Christ our Saviour and his work of salvation with us.

Guidelines

The aim of the conference is to develop the spiritual understanding and commitment of those attending and strengthen friendships around the Word of God. Our fellowship is based on the Word of God and living a life according to it.

In all ways we must show ourselves to be followers of Christ and so leave behind a good impression that will be a testimony to The Truth and bring glory to God.

  1. The basis of fellowship at the conference is the BASF, extended to members of the Central Christadelphian Fellowship.
  2. Instructions and decisions of the hosts are to be respected.
  3. Please attend all organised activities on time so that the conference can run smoothly.
  4. Attendees must remain within the precincts of the conference premises except when specific permission to leave has been granted by one of the hosts.
  5. Dress should be respectful for the sessions and modest in general. Formal attire is preferred for the memorial meeting and head coverings are considered scriptural for sisters.
  6. Modest shorts/pants are allowed for females during recreational afternoon activities. Everyone is expected to dress tidily for the evening meal and subsequent activities.
  7. Men are not allowed in ladies’ rooms and vice-versa.
  8. Electronic devices are expected to be used for Bible study activities.
  9. Please do not bring alcohol, tobacco or other recreational drugs to the conference.
